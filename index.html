Salsa

Haydee Sanchez

Dance is a very important part of the cultures of many Latin American countries. Salsa is one of the traditional dances shared by many cultures of Latyin America.

Salsa is the traditional dance of Puerto Rico, Cuba, and Cali-Colombia. Salsa is a partner dance that origins from Afro-Cuban culture. When performing, dancers must wear colorful costumes. These usually include a lot of rhinestones, feathers, and fringes. Salsa is danced in 8 counts and requires two people. One is the leader and the other is the follow. The lead is the person that turns and spins the follow.

Salsa is now danced around the world. There are many festivals to celebrate the Caribbean culture. Events like the International Salsa Congress and Chicago Salsa and Bachata Festival is celebrated in Chicago
